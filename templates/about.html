What is SoftnetAfrica School Management System?

SoftnetAfrica is a powerful and modern School Management System built to digitalize and simplify daily operations in Liberian high schools. Our platform enables administrators, teachers, students, and parents to work seamlessly through a secure and efficient digital environment.

  • Smart Attendance Tracking
  • Digital Gradebook and Report Cards
  • Online Events & Announcement Management
  • Student, Teacher, and Parent Dashboards
  • Finance & Tuition Tracking with Transparency
